Our philosophy

The principles that guide our every move

When you’re a company of our size, you have to have a strong set of beliefs.

So, without further ado, here are ours.

Drive your Ambition

Drive your Ambition is a powerful statement of Mitsubishi Motors Corporation's (MMC) ongoing commitment to the values and aspirations of our drivers.


We like to be first. We like to be best


We are pioneers. We’ve been creating innovative vehicles for over 100 years. In 1982, we launched the Pajero/Montero. It was the pioneer of a new generation of SUVs that offered sophisticated driving performance at a reasonable price. In 2009, we launched the i-MiEV, the world’s first pure electric car available for the mass market. In 2013, we launched the Outlander PHEV, the world’s first Plug-in Hybrid SUV. We are here to do things that have never been done before. We are dedicated to the advancement of the automotive industry.

We are specialists in electrification technologies

We will continue to develop Electric Vehicle technology. We’ll create cars that offer smoother, quieter rides with powerful acceleration that can travel further on a single charge.

Our designs are rooted our philosophy of robustness and dependability. And we have lofty ambitions: we want our designs to be nothing short of ingenious.

They are created with meticulous care and precision and we focus on getting the details right. We call this Mitsubishi-ness.



We will never stop innovating

When we launched the Outlander PHEV in 2013, it was the world's first Plug-in Hybrid SUV. We weren't just way ahead of our competitors, the Outlander PHEV represented more than that. It offered a new kind of low cost, low carbon driving. It won Innovation Award in the Car of the Year Japan, 2013-2014. The worldwide attention it received helped to firmly establish the importance of Plug-in Hybrid electric vehicles as the way forward.

For over 100 years, we've been developing cutting-edge auto technology. But we’re not finished yet, not by a long way. Using our expert knowledge in EV technology and 4X4 systems and by further integrating advanced digital technologies into our vehicles such as AI and IOT, we’re already looking at the next 100 years to create cars that are cleaner, safer and more enjoyable for you to drive.



The future of mobility

We've been creating cutting-edge vehicle technology for more than a century. However, by no means are we done yet. We're already planning for the next 100 years to develop automobiles that are cleaner, safer, and more pleasant for you to drive by utilizing our extensive knowledge of EV technology, 4X4 systems, and further integrating cutting-edge digital technologies like AI and IOT into our vehicles.

Everything we do is rooted in our heritage of honesty, robustness, dependability and Japanese craftsmanship. We’re pioneers when it comes to technology, designing and creating ground-breaking SUV and electric (EV) technologies. Take a closer look at our designs and latest work.

Our PLUG-IN HYBRID EV(PHEV) system is an advanced series hybrid with twin motors. This revolutionary technology could transform your daily commute. Because the generating and storage of electricity is so efficient, you'll have enough power that you won't need to fill up with petrol nearly so often. See how a PHEV can drastically reduce your running costs.
